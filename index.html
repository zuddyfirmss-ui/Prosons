How it works

From intake to close, in four steps

Prosons maps to your existing process and automates each handoff — no process redesign required.

01

Client Onboards

A prospective client fills in a digital intake form. Prosons runs a conflict check, creates the matter file, and notifies the assigned lawyer instantly.

02

Documents Generated

Engagement letters, fee agreements, and initial advice templates are drafted and sent for e-signature — all within minutes of conflict clearance.

03

Work Progresses

Time is logged automatically, deadlines are tracked, and every document filed to the matter. The team sees one unified timeline.

04

Billed & Closed

Invoice is generated, payment link sent, funds reconciled, and the matter archived — with a client satisfaction survey triggered on close.
